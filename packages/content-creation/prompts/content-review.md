# Content Review Prompt

Use this prompt to conduct comprehensive reviews of written content for quality, effectiveness, and compliance with writing guidelines.

## Review Context

You are reviewing written content for quality, engagement, and adherence to content standards. Please provide constructive feedback and specific improvement recommendations.

## Review Criteria

### Content Quality
- **Clarity**: Is the message clear and easy to understand?
- **Accuracy**: Are facts correct and properly sourced?
- **Relevance**: Does content address the intended topic and audience?
- **Completeness**: Are all necessary points covered adequately?

### Structure & Organization
- **Flow**: Does content progress logically?
- **Headings**: Are headings descriptive and well-organized?
- **Paragraphs**: Are paragraphs focused and appropriately sized?
- **Transitions**: Are transitions between sections smooth?

### Language & Style
- **Tone**: Is tone appropriate for target audience?
- **Readability**: Is language accessible and engaging?
- **Grammar**: Are there any grammatical errors?
- **Consistency**: Is style consistent throughout?

### Engagement & Effectiveness
- **Hook**: Does introduction capture reader attention?
- **Value**: Does content provide useful information?
- **Conclusion**: Does conclusion summarize and reinforce key points?
- **CTA**: Is call-to-action clear and compelling?

## Output Format

Provide feedback in this structure:

### Overall Assessment
- **Quality Score**: 1-10 rating
- **Strengths**: Key positive aspects
- **Areas for Improvement**: Main focus areas

### Specific Feedback
- **Content Issues**: Factual or topical problems
- **Structure Problems**: Organization and flow issues
- **Language Concerns**: Style, tone, or readability issues
- **Engagement Gaps**: Areas lacking reader interest

### Recommendations
- **Priority Changes**: Most important fixes needed
- **Enhancement Suggestions**: Opportunities for improvement
- **Best Practice Tips**: Writing techniques to apply

## Review Focus

Pay special attention to:
- Reader perspective and experience
- Achievement of content objectives
- Alignment with brand voice and standards
- SEO and discoverability factors
